Welcome to the unique culinary world of our restaurant, a place where the richness of French gastronomy merges harmoniously with the passion of Italian cuisine.
Guided by the vision of renowned Franco-Italian Chef Giuseppe Martinelli , our team strives to create an exceptional taste experience that transcends borders.

At Bella Vita, the fusion of French and Italian culinary techniques gives rise to a menu that reflects the best of both worlds. The main courses serve as a testament to the culinary expertise on display. From the comforting embrace of Coq au Vin from France to the fabulous Risotto ai Funghi Porcini from Italy.

Our signature dishes

COQ AU VIN
Immerse yourself in a harmony of flavours with our interpretation of the French classic, "Coq au Vin." The rooster, slow-cooked in a red wine reduction, marries French elegance with hints of Italian herbs, creating a symphony of flavours that will awaken your taste buds.

RISOTTO AI FUNGHI PORCINI
Experience the Italian touch with Risotto ai Funghi Porcini, a creamy and earthy risotto infused with the intense aroma of porcini mushrooms. Each bite is a celebration of Italian simplicity and elegance. Immerse yourself in the rich flavors of Northern Italy as you savor the velvety texture and distinctive taste of this classic dish.

INGREDIENTS AND TECHNIQUES IN EVIDENCE
Indulge us as we unfold a culinary narrative, delicately unveiling the essence of our dishes. Rather than revealing every detail, we prefer to offer a glimpse into the artistry that defines our menu, a harmonious blend of two cultures, paying homage to the rich history of our esteemed chef. This endeavor is a meticulous interplay of techniques and ingredients, carefully curated to create an extraordinary dining experience.
Consider, for instance, our homage to the marriage of sauces in the exquisite "Bouillabaisse." Here, refined French sauces entwine gracefully with Italian influences, crafting a symphony of distinctive and captivating flavors.
Journey further into the Mediterranean with the inclusion of Provençal herbs in the classic "Ratatouille." This infusion imparts a sun-kissed essence, elevating traditional French cuisine to new heights.
Explore the realm of Italian culinary finesse with the preparation of "Polenta con Tartufo Nero." A showcase of time-honored Italian techniques, this dish introduces a velvety texture and the alluring aroma of black truffle to its accompaniments, promising a truly sublime dining experience.

Side dishes,drinks and desserts

Side dishes are like delicious sidekicks that complete the main meal. Take "Ratatouille" for example, a colourful celebration of fresh French vegetables, prepared with Mediterranean savoir-faire.

Our selection of drinks reflects the perfect union between renowned French vineyards and the Italian aperitif tradition. Dive into elegance with "Chianti Classico," an Italian red wine that perfectly accompanies our dishes, or opt for the sparkling sophistication of "Champagne." If you're looking for a more Italian experience, "Negroni" takes you on a journey of balanced bitterness, a true Mediterranean experience in a bottle.

Finally, round off your meal with divine sweets that fuse French delicacy with Italian passion. Tiramisu," a revisited Italian classic, offers creamy layers of French mascarpone and robust coffee. Discover also the "Crème Brûlée," where French simplicity is sublimated by Italian touches, creating a sweet harmony that will seduce the most demanding palates. Each dessert is a sweet dance that celebrates the gourmet alliance of two exceptional pastry-making traditions.

To embark on a culinary journey beyond this preview, we invite you to join us in person for a refined dining experience or opt for the convenience of delivery, bringing the opulence of our cuisine to the comfort of your home. We eagerly anticipate the pleasure of welcoming you, where our chef's passionate artistry awaits your discerning palate.
